Getting Started with House
of Hamsters

House of Hamsters is a blockchain game that rewards players for their
smart decision-making. Play Texas Hold’em and earn cryptocurrency.

01 Register

Download the House of Hamsters game from Google Play or the App Store
on your smartphone and create an account.

02 Set Up Your Wallet

After registration, set up your wallet within the app.

03 Get a Free Hamster and Try to Play

Right after sign-up, enjoy a complimentary non-NFT character, Hamster,
and an energy boost. Now you can immerse yourself in the app, take on
energy tournaments, and challenge rivals.

04 Start making $HAMC

Secure top-three spots in energy tournaments for rewarding $HAMC token
earnings.

05 Boost Earnings with NFTs

Enhance energy tournament gains through NFT characters or attributes.

$HAMC

$HAMC is a token created on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC) with an
unlimited total emission, functioning as a digital asset operating on the
BSC blockchain. $HAMC serves as the key token within the house of
Hamsters ecosystem, managing the in-game economy and ensuring its
stability, enabling players to monetize their activities and play to earn.

Transparency

House of Hamsters uses the cutting-edge
power of blockchain technology to
document all transactions in a
transparent, public ledger. Thanks to the
open source shuffling and dealing of
cards, we provide a completely open and
fair competition.

Roadmap

Our vision extends beyond traditional boundaries. House of Hamsters
offers a unique blend of thrilling poker gameplay and the potential to earn
cryptocurrency rewards.

1st and 2nd Quarter

Market research on NFT games and poker. Game concept formation, defining key features and mechanics.
Tokenomics and game economic model creation.
Mini-game development to attract audience and test gameplay
mechanics.
Initiation of RNG model development with open-source code to
ensure fairness of gameplay
Launching mini-game for gathering feedback and assessing
audience interest.
Integration with Solana blockchain for NFT asset creation and
management.
Development and testing of smart contracts for secure and
transparent NFT trading.
Blockchain wallet development for storing and managing NFT
assets.
Optimization of on-chain transactions to enhance network speed
and efficiency.
Genesis NFT collection sales round to attract investors and
collectors.
Minimum Viable Product (MVP) poker game development with
basic features and NFT asset management.
Main game testing among a limited audience to identify bugs and
flaws.

3rd and 4th Quarter

Feedback analysis and user interface and gameplay optimization
based on testing results. Addition of extra poker game variations and modes to increase
player diversity and engagement.
Smart contract audit to ensure their security and reliability.
Integration with Biswap DEX to enhance accessibility and liquidity
of game tokens.
Launch of internal NFT marketplace for trading and exchange of
unique assets.
Commencement of mobile application development to expand
audience reach and usability.
Commencement of NFT table sales for poker, providing unique
gaming opportunities and advantages.
